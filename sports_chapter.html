Volleyball

A good team can win a volleyball game when they are ahead, a great team can win when they are behind. 28. Don't let the ball hit the ground without a body hitting the ground with it.
Volleyball

Registration Fees: ₹800

Prize money worth ₹4500

General Details:

  • It is a knockout tournament.
  • Matches will be of 3 sets of 15 points each.
  • Finals will be of 3 sets of 25 points each.
  • All standard FIVB rules (except for serve, rotation and libero) are applicable.
  • All students must carry a student-identity card.
  • Decision of the match officials is final and teams are supposed to co-operate with them.
  • Unsporting behavior by any player on or off the field will be dealt with strictly and may result in expulsion of his team even.
  • Further rules will be explained to the team captains before start of a match.

The organisers reserve the right to change any of the rules, timings and other details such as prize money at any point of time. In case of any dispute, the decision of the organisers is final.
