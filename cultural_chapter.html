Nikkana (Solo Dance)

Your body is an instrument that you play to the symphony of dance. This Solo-Dance event has provided year-after-year a stage for the amalgam of Indian and Western Dance categories, where top 20 participants will out-perform each other in Indian/Western Category to lose themselves to this symphony of Dance.

Nikkana (Solo Dance)

Registration Fees:

  • ₹100 for Team size 1.
  • ₹150 for Team size 2

Prize Money worth ₹5000.

General Rules: These are the rules for western / easter solo both...

  1. Team size is either 1 or 2.
  2. Participants must reach registration desk at least 30 minutes before the scheduled time of the event.(i.e. within 8:30 am)
  3. Each participant must bring school or college id-card in order to register for this event.
  4. No props will be provided from us. Participants are requested not to use any prop or act which may cause harm to themselves or anyone present in the auditorium.
  5. Each team (of size 1 or 2) will be allotted MAX 3 minutes to perform.
  6. Fusion Dance is allowed.
  7. Vulgarity of any kind would lead to instant disqualification from the event. Hence, if the participant feels that the outfit / performance can be deemed as vulgar, it is strongly advised to consult the organizers before putting it on stage. The decision of the organizer will be final in case of any disputes arising due to this reason.
  8. The stage must be cleared of all props after every performance by the participant within 2 minute.
  9. A team will be alloted maximum 5 minutes within which they have to perform and clear all props, violation of which will be resulted into deducting marks and even disqualification from the competition.
  10. Participants more than 14 yrs will be allowed to participate. (Age as on 31st Jan 2019)

Band-E-Thlon

With the best campus bands in the country fighting each other, Band-e-thlon promises to be one of the most exciting shows of Integration.
Band-E-Thlon

After a huge success last year, Band-e-thlon invites musical bands from all across the country to come and fight for the coveted Band-of-the-Year Award. Acoustic Drum kit will be provided for the finals. The event will be held on the 2nd of Feburary, Saturday. The participating bands must take their respective slot numbers from the registration desk on the 2nd of Feburary, Saturday. Details on this will be provided via e-mail to the selected bands. Lots of attractive goodies and cash prize at stake.

Steps to Register:

  1. Every band has to send a mail to bandethlon@gmail.com specifying their band name and the contact number of two of their members.
  2. The time limit for bands to send their tracks is 11:59pm, 12th January, 2019.
  3. They must necessarily send two of their own recorded tracks to bandethlon@gmail.com for a preliminary scrutiny. On the basis of these tracks, some bands will be selected for the final round. The selected bands will be informed via e-mail by 16th January 2019.
  4. Selected bands will have to pay registration fees of ₹700 (per band) to the organizers at the Band-e-thelon registration desk by 20th January 2019.
  5. The registration of a particular band shall be considered incomplete if they fail to pay the registration fees by the last date, even if their place is confirmed in the final round.(Each band member must have a student identity card)

Rules for Last round:

  • No music track or computed beat can be used.
  • The stage performance should be minimum 10 minutes long and the band should perform at least 2 songs.
  • Sound check should be done within 5 minutes which will be considered from the time the previous band gets down from stage.
  • Cigarettes or other inflammable items are not allowed on stage.
  • Each band must perform at least one original number.
  • The organizers reserve the right to stop a band in the midst of their performance in case the band misbehaves on stage.
  • All the band members must produce valid photo-identity cards.
  • The judges' decision will be taken as final.

Total prize money worth ₹8000

Montage

General Rules:

  • Each participant must submit ONE disc (CD/ DVD/ Blu-ray) which won't be returned to the participant.
  • In the main directory of the submitted disc, there should be a folder called "Montage 2018". Any other file or folder in the main directory will be ignored.
  • In the "Montage 2018" folder of the main directory of the submitted disc, there should be two files naming - "entry.avi" and "info.txt"- the absence of either resulting in direct disqualification; a file called "entry.srt- if found - will be recognized; everything else in the folder will be ignored.

Thru-da-Lens

"Life is like photography. We develop from the negatives"
Thru-da-Lens

Integration 2019 brings you this once in a lifetime opportunity to showcase your talent, leave alone the hi chance to win a total prize worth ₹5,000. and other exciting goodies. Submit your photographs asap.

This is one of the most coveted photography competitions of Eastern India. Niepce (B&W) and Daguerre (Colour). Those who want to participate, please send your photographs to this mail id: photography.integration@gmail.com

IMPORTANT DATES:

  • Photo Submission Deadline: 16th January, 2019
  • Announcement of result of prelims: 20th January, 2019
  • Date of registration of candidates for the final round of judging: 25th January, 2019
  • Exhibition: 2nd,3rd February, 2019
  • Final Result Declaration: 2nd February, 2019

General Rules:

  • There would be two groups for this competition:
    1. The Black & White variant (Niepce)
    2. Color photographs (Daguerre)
    There is no restriction on topics for the two variants and no editing other than cropping, color adjustment & brightness and contrast corrections, straightening and spot removal are allowed.
  • Each participant can submit at most 2 (two) photographs (soft copy) per group, in JPEG/TIFF format, 10" by 15" in size, resolution 300 dpi. File size of a photograph should not exceed 10 MB. Submit only formatted print ready photographs. Note that photos submitted in previous Thru-da-Lens competitions will not be considered for judging.
  • A caption/write-up in not more than 50 words, in MS word .doc format MUST BE ACCOMPANIED with each photograph, along with the submitter's name, email id, phone no., photograph legend, camera model, technical specifications, date, time & place of snap shot.
  • Watermarking may be allowed. Computer generated images will not be accepted, only images originating as camera taken will be considered. Image manipulations are not allowed except simple editing (those mentioned in Rule 1).
  • The photographs are to be mailed to photography.integration@gmail.com. The last date for sending the photographs is 16th January, 2019.
  • The best 30 entries from each group will be considered for final round of competition and will be displayed during 2nd-3rd February, 2019. This first round of judging will be done by 20th January, 2019, after which qualifying participants can register. Judges for the first round are: "To be announced soon..."
  • The senders of the selected photographs will be notified through email and phone. The registration fee for the selected photographs will be Rs. 100/- each and must be done by 25th January, (12 noon).
  • Only duly registered photographs will be considered for the final judgment.
  • The final judging and announcement of results will be done by 20th January 2019 by a panel of Judges.
  • From each group, there would be two winning photographs. The prizes are worth Rs 1500 (First Prize), Rs 1000 (Second Prize) on each category.
